Website of Dr. Rhea-Silvia Remus

I am an Astrophysicist working on the formation and evolution of galaxies. Currently, I am a scientific researcher at the University Observatory in Munich, after being back from my research visit at the CITA in Toronto. My research is both theoretical and computational, and I am part of the Magneticum Pathfinder team at the University Observatory in Munich and leader of the working group DRAGONS. Here you can find information about me and my research.
