I am a passionate Physics Major primarily focusing on Theoretical Astro-particle physics and observational Cosmology. I am currently working on the formation and evolution of galaxies. Currently, I am a Masters Student at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in Mumbai, after being back from my research visit at the Max Planck Institute for Physics (MPP), Munich, Germany.

My work is both theoretical and computational, and I am part of the Magneticum Pathfinder team at the University Observatory in Munich and leader of the working group DRAGONS. Here you can find information about me and my research.
